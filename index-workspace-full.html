Materials & Quality

Engineered for Safety.
Built for Views.

The materials behind every installation — specified for architects, trusted by builders.

🪟 Tempered Safety Glass

½" (12mm) standard — 4x stronger than regular glass. SentryGlas® ionoplast interlayer available for high-elevation and structural applications. Exceeds IBC/CRC safety requirements.

⚓ Marine-Grade Hardware

AISI 316 stainless steel hardware — corrosion-resistant, maintenance-free. Suitable for oceanfront, poolside, and coastal installations. Also available in 304-grade for interior applications.

💎 Low-Iron Starphire® Option

Removes the green tint found in standard glass. True-to-color transparency for ocean and view properties. Starphire® by Vitro — the clearest architectural glass available.
